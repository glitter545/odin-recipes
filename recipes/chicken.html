Chicken

This easy Italian dressing marinade for chicken breasts is a simple but delicious way to add flavor before grilling.

Italian Chciken Marinade

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions

  1. Whisk salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together in a shallow baking dish; add chicken breasts and turn to coat. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator, 4 hours to overnight.
  2. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess; discard remaining marinade.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
